About me

Hi, I’m Ludvig.

I’m a fourth year computer security student at Bleking Tekniska Högskola in Karlskrona.

I like Cryptography, analysing malware, playing CTF’s, organizing CTF’s, playing golf, drinking alcohol, eating GOOD pizza and having a laugh.

So, altough I’m only 23, I sound like a 45 year old.

This blog will mostly be about my findings and notes about cryptograhy, both implementations and analysis. Most things will not be new or revolutionary, but it’s a fun way for me to take notes on what I learn on the way.
